Master Skills List

Ray's Whirld uses a percentage-based system. While based on classic archetypes, mastery requires navigating complex prerequisite chains and specialized training. Sources include GURPS and Rifts skill lists

Skill Mechanics

Skill Base: 5% × Level + (Score - 15) if Score > 15.
Jack o' Trades: 1% Per Level + 1/5 of any Bonus to attempt any skill (must meet prerequisites).
Critical Success: Roll ≤ 1/10 of target percentage.
