Skills

From Acting to Zoology — if it isn't combat, there's a skill for it. Ray's Whirld uses the GURPS skill list as its base, adapted to the RW percentage system.

Skill Mechanics

Skill Base = 5% × Level (Level 1–10 = 5–50% Base)
Score Bonus = +5% per point above 15 of the relevant score
Critical Success = Roll ≤ 1/10 of target %
📋 Example: Swimming at Level 10 / Con 20
50% Base + 25% Con Bonus = 75% to swim in stormy seas. No roll needed for calm water — rolls only required for challenge situations.

  • Rural Survival — Essential outside of cities

  • Literacy — Prerequisite for Tec Use
  • Acrobatics + Gymnastics — Both required to take Dodging (+2 Def)
