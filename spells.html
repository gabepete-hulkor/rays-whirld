Spell Stacking

Spells may be "stacked" for greater power. Spells like Intelligence, Dexterity, Bless, Pray, Improve, etc. work together to create a more powerful combined effect.

✨ Example: Blessed Command
A Blessed Command is twice as powerful as an unbuffed Command. Stacking buff spells before combat or before key actions is a core strategic element of Ray's Whirld.

Bard Songs

Bards use Music Magic. Key rules:

  • Casting Time: [Spell Level]² ÷ Bard Level minutes to play
  • Song Power: Bard Level + Bonus by Charisma
  • Not usually suitable for use in melee combat
  • Played before encounters, songs can last longer than their casting time or be delayed until needed
🎵 Team Song
A team song can be composed (2–5 minutes long, or 1 minute per team member divided by Bard Level). Each team member receives a total bonus equal to the Bard's Song Power, usable 1 point at a time or all at once on a single roll.
