Classes

Ray's Whirld uses modified classic AD&D classes alongside its own unique additions. Multi-classing is possible but comes with significant costs.

Classic Classes (AD&D circa 1978, Modified)

Fighter
"Mostly I Just Hit Things"
Prime Scores (16 min): Strength, Dexterity, Constitution
Alignment: Any
Prof: 4  |  Skills: 2

✦ Weapons, Hit Points, Armor (best options)

✦ Attack bonus: +5 to Atk/Def

✗ Skills (fewest), Spells (none)

Paladin
"For Justice!"
Prime Scores (17 min): Str, Dex, Con, Wisdom
Secondary (15 min): Charisma
Alignment: Lawful Good Only
Prof: —  |  Skills: 2

✦ Spell use, Special Abilities, Respect

✦ Can Requisition Equipment, Attack bonus: +4

✗ Restrictions (Lawful Good, Celibate, Donate 10% to the Federation of Law), Equipment Limits ("Taxes")

Ranger
"I'm Covering"
Prime Scores (16 min): Strength, Intelligence, Wisdom, Constitution
Alignment: Always Good (Any)

✦ Animal friends, Rural Skills, Druid & Mage Spells

✦ Attack bonus: +4

✗ Less effective in urban settings

"In The Name of God!"

Secondary (15 min): Charisma
Alignment: Any (by chosen deity)

✦ Good Armor, Spells (Wisdom-based)

✦ Attack bonus: +3

"I'll Take That!"

Assassin
"Die!"
Prime Scores (16 min): Dexterity, Intelligence, Sight

✦ Attack bonus: +1

Jedi
"Use The Force!"
All Scores (17 min): Luck, Str, Dex, Con, Wis, Intel
Alignment: Any Lawful
Light = Good, Dark = Evil, Grey = Neutral
Must take: Technologist Subclass

✦ "Use The Force!", Powerful, Federation of Law Support

✦ All Exp, HP, Prof, Skills as Paladin

✗ Limited duration, All restrictions of Paladins

Force is more "psychic" than elemental (ESP, Suggest, Mental Blast). First power must be "Force Sense."

Bard
"The Song Goes On..."
Prerequisites: Fighter, Thief (or more) + Musician Subclass at Level 10 in all 3
Subclass: Musician (required)

✦ Music Magic, Magic Items at half power (mage/cleric) or full power (fighter/thief)

✦ Team Song: grants party bonuses up to Song Power to each member

✗ Must quest for Bard class training, Attack bonus: 0

Spell/Songs take [Spell Lvl]² ÷ Bard Level minutes to play.

Subclasses

Any subclass gives –1 to Attack and Defence per subclass taken. Subclasses are in addition to a primary class.

SubclassMinimum ScoreBenefitsPenalty
Alchemist Intelligence 17 ID Potions, Make Potions, Carry More Potions, More Skills (Start w/5+) –1 Atk & Def
Technologist Intelligence 16 ID Technology, Bonus (Tec) Skills. Required for Jedi class. –1 Atk & Def
Musician Dexterity & Hearing 16 Bonus Instrument Skills. Required for Bard class. –1 Atk & Def (to level 10)
Cook — "OK" — minor utility benefits –1 Atk & Def

Multi-Classing

⚠ Multi-Class Costs
  • Slower advancement
  • Fewer HP (versus best class)
  • More expensive training and equipment
  • Harder score requirements (must meet all classes' minimums)
  • Average Hit Dice across classes
  • All abilities and all restrictions of every class apply

Total Attack/Defence Level is calculated from the total level of all prime classes (not subclasses). Example: Fighter/Mage Level 3/3 = 6 total levels = ±0 modifier.
