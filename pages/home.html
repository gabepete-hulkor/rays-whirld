Welcome to the Whirld

Ray's Whirld is an Extreme Fantasy Sci-fi Game World drawing ideas from a large variety of games, movies, and comics. This wiki is your complete reference for rules, lore, classes, magic, and more.

⚡ Ray's Whirld Law
"Advanced Magic may resemble Technology." — Contrast with Clarke's Law: "Technology sufficiently advanced resembles Magic."

What Makes Ray's Whirld Unique

💪
Powerful PCs
Characters are made to be powerful and unique. Multi-classing, mutations, special abilities, and superhuman attribute scores are all possible.
✨
Lör Magic System
A unique elemental magic system inspired by the Thomas Covenant series and Avatar: The Last Airbender. Point-based, risky, and powerful.
⚔️
Complex Combat
A sophisticated d20 system with Attack vs. Defence differentials, fumble tables, dodge mechanics, and damage modifiers by AC.
🧙
Rich Classes
Classic AD&D classes heavily modified, plus Lör Workers, Jedi, Bards, and multiple subclass options like Alchemist and Technologist.
🌍
Vast World
Surface area over 30× Earth. Includes extreme caverns, exo-planets, post-apocalyptic regions, and space. Anything can exist here.
🎭
Grudge System
Earn and spend "Grudge" points — powerful meta-currency for character creation, die re-rolls, and improving your odds at critical moments.
— ✦ —

Source Material

📚Core Sources
  • AD&D (1st Edition) — Base classes, spell lists, combat foundation
  • D&D 3.5e — Additional rules and mechanics
  • GURPS — Technology levels and gear tables
  • Unearthed Arcana — Extended spell lists (Type B spells)
  • Rifts — Gonzo Lists
  • Warhammer 40K — Critters
🎬Inspirations
  • Avatar: The Last Airbender — Lör element system
  • Thomas Covenant Series — Original Lör inspiration
  • Star Wars — The Force / Jedi class
  • Comics, movies, games — Vast pop-culture crossover
⚠ Be Warned
Ray's Whirld can be extremely deadly and dangerous. Post-apocalyptic death worlds, NPCs like Dracula and Doctor Doom, carnivorous plants, environmental traps — death is common. That's what Grudge is for. Track your victories and defeats!
