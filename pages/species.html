Lizardfolk

A well-documented species in Ray's Whirld lore. See source: Forgotten Realms Fandom Wiki (adapted).

Size
Medium (6–7 ft, 200–250 lbs)
Alignment
Usually Neutral
Vision
Darkvision
Lifespan
Up to 80 years
Language
Draconic, Common
Deity
Semuanya

Abilities

  • Strong and hardy; thick scaly hide provides natural protection
  • Can hold breath far longer than humans (up to twice as long)
  • Tail aids balance, leaping, and swimming
  • Darkvision
  • Claws and teeth can be used as weapons

Personality & Culture

Lizardfolk have little interest in money or jewels, and value things primarily based on whether they are useful or edible. They are not inherently evil — simply savage with a hard time fitting into civilized society. They refer to most civilized races as "softskins."

Society is tribal and patriarchal, organized by strength and power. Tribal shamans are usually clerics worshiping Semuanya.
