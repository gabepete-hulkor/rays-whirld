Mutations & Special Abilities

Everyone in Ray's Whirld is descended from mutant stock. Mutations are gifts (and sometimes curses) that make every character truly unique. Choose 1–5 mutations at character creation — 1 in 5 is a Defect. Use Grudge to re-roll or improve.

🎲 Mutation Rules

Mutations are powered by Luck. More Luck = more powerful mutations. Roll randomly or use a template (costs Grudge). Every 5th mutation roll is a Defect — a drawback that balances the power. Roll 1d500 — or let the DM choose.

Ray's Whirld Mutations (Character-Specific)

🔮 Illusion Generation

Effect: Create superior illusions with movement, sound, and images. Can run 2 separate illusions simultaneously doing different things.

Static Illusion: Int minimum check to maintain

Dynamic Illusion: Con of actions check & requires focus

5×/day at 200% skill

📐 Size Change

Grow: Str = 50 × sizes over Medium. Max Str scales with XL/XXL etc.

Shrink: Dex = 10 × sizes under Medium. Gain stealth bonuses.

Duration: ½ hour per Con point

Gear: Can size-change up to 20kg of equipment

8×/day

👁️ Invisibility

Effect: Full invisibility. Stacks with Hide skill (200% effective).

Duration: Con minutes per use

15×/day

💪 Will Force

Effect: Temporarily double a single stat (×2).

Duration: 20 actions

Stacks: Yes — can apply multiple times

4×/day

🎭 Shape Change (Skrull)

Effect: Change species appearance entirely, including voice, scent, and size (within 1 size category).

Bonus: +1 to Disguise and Impersonation skills while shifted

2×/Level/Day

🦴 Improved Bones

Natural AC: AC 5, +10 defense (¼ damage)

vs. Sonic: +15 defense (1/5 damage)

vs. Blunt: +15 defense (1/5 damage)

Passive — always active

🪱 Elastic Body Parts

Extend: Stretch limbs/body up to 120% of normal length

Compress: Compress down to 60% of normal size

Duration: Con + BBLevel per day, in minutes each

OZ Boost: +220% extend, 80% compress, +6/day, +20 min each

Stretch-Dodge bonus stacks!

⚡ Cosmic Energy Absorption

Effect: Absorb incoming energy attacks (lasers, spells, electricity) and re-channel them.

Limit: 2 rolls per rest period

Excellent vs. energy-heavy enemies

🔋 Energize Item

Effect: Channel energy into an object — vastly increasing its attack power.

Bonus: +40 ATK, additional 20d10 damage

Duration: Con actions (e.g., 20 actions)

50 uses/day

🌍 Tremorsense

Effect: Detect movement and location through ground vibrations.

Range: Specified in score (e.g., Tremor 20 = 50-hour detection window)

Passive ability, no action cost

🎭 Project Illusion

Effect: Project a convincing illusion of a specific creature or person (e.g., Klingon form) around yourself.

Duration: Con minutes per use

5×/day

⚠️ Defects (Examples)

Dietary Deficiency: –1 Nish/day without supplement; –20 Lor/day

Hated By Demonic Entity: Powerful enemy. Be careful at night...

Initiative Dependency: Only gain Init bonus in narrow temperature range (e.g., 80–100°F)

Stat Vulnerability: –5 vs. Hypnotism, –3 vs. Gaze attacks, etc.

Kiyap

🥋 Kiyap — The Battle Cry

A powerful shout that boosts attack. Standard Kiyap gives +1 to attack. With the Bruce Lee chip: +5 Kiyap standard, +10 Kiyap once per day. Can be used every other round unless chipped. Stacks with other bonuses.
